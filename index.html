Get to know me

Born in May 27th, 1995, Fábio lived his whole life in Belo Horizonte/MG, Brazil, where he studied on Marista Dom Silvério, a traditional local school of the city. Right after high school, he decided to start a graduation on Chemical Engineering, on one of the country's most illustrious universities: UFMG (Universidade Federal de Minas Gerais).


During his graduation, Fábio had the opportunity of escaping the usual "thecnical only" formation and further developing his personal skills, working as a salesman specialist on two separated occasions, both directly in contact with every leading figure and CEO of the companies. Proudly achieved 7 revenue generation records and stablished a new way of dealing with leads. In a start-up growing 100+% each month, was responsible for generating more than R$ 100.000,00 in revenue and held 13% of all company's income during the working period.


While setting records and becoming a top-performer in the field,completed his graduation as a Chemical Engineer and decided to learn how to code to automate regular tasks but, in the end, fell in love with everything tech-related and could not bear the idea of not working with that. Decided, then, to drop out of sales and is now attending a 1-year full-stack course, to better understand and work on the field.


This website was created in order to share Fábio's most relavant projects and ideas, while also listing relavant stuff about the field.
